Our Story

Welcome to
Minnie's Farm Resort

A peaceful sanctuary where nature, comfort, and genuine hospitality come together for an unforgettable escape.

A Family Dream
Turned Into Reality

Welcome to Minnie's Farm Resort, a hidden gem in the peaceful town of Basud, Camarines Norte. Our story began with a simple idea: to create a place where families and friends could escape the hustle of city life and reconnect with nature.

Founded by Maximana Morales and her family, the resort has grown from a humble farmhouse into a full retreat that blends rustic charm with modern comfort.

Today, we welcome guests from all over to experience the simple joys of farm life — morning harvests, open skies, and home-cooked meals made from our very own garden.

More Than a Resort —
It's a Home Away

Forget the typical hotel stay; Minnie's Farm Resort is all about the great outdoors and good times.

Our rooms are thoughtfully designed to reflect the warmth of a family home — wooden accents, cozy bedding, and windows that frame the natural beauty surrounding the property.

We believe every guest deserves to feel like family, and that's exactly how we treat everyone who stays with us.
