Privacy Policy

Minnie's Farm Resort

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Minnie's Farm Resort ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the resort booking platform. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (hashed)
  • Authentication Data: OAuth identifiers (Google ID, Facebook ID)
  • Booking Information: Room selections, dates, pricing, confirmation details
  • Review Data: Text reviews, star ratings you submit
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information (via logs)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process your room bookings and payments
  • Send confirmation emails and notifications
  • Allow you to leave reviews and ratings
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: Sign-in authentication (Google handles your Google account data)
  • Facebook OAuth: Sign-in authentication (Facebook handles your Facebook account data)
  • SendGrid: Email delivery service
  • Render.com: Backend hosting
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in our MySQL database on Render.com
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
  • Your connection uses HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth tokens are handled securely per OAuth providers' standards

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as you have an active account. Booking history is kept for 7 years (for legal/tax purposes). After account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your account data
  • Correction: You can update your information
  • Deletion: You can request account and data deletion
  • Portability: You can request your data in a standard format

8. User Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and personal data:

  1. Via Email: Send a request to moralesmickdaniel7@gmail.com
  2. Include:
    • Your account email address
    • A brief explanation (optional)
  3. Timeline: We will process your deletion within 30 days and confirm via email

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your account name, email, and password
  • Your bookings (permanently removed from active records)
  • Your reviews and ratings
  • Your OAuth identifiers
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

What We Keep (for legal compliance):

  • Anonymized booking records for 7 years (tax/legal requirement)
  • Archived system logs (for security only, no personal identifiers)
